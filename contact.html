Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a stay?

Simply search for your destination, select your dates and number of guests, then choose from our curated selection of properties. Click on any property to view details and complete your booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies by property. Most bookings can be cancelled free of charge up to 24 hours before check-in. Check the specific property details for exact cancellation terms.

Do you offer 24/7 customer support?

Yes! Our customer support team is available 24/7 via live chat, email, and phone to assist you with any questions or issues during your stay.

How do I modify my booking?

You can modify your booking by logging into your account or contacting our support team. Changes are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and various digital wallets. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees! The price you see includes all taxes and service fees. Any additional charges (like cleaning fees) are clearly displayed before you complete your booking.
