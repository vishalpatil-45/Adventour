Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a package?

Simply browse our packages, select your preferred destination, click "Book Now", and follow the step-by-step booking process. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately after completing your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refund if cancelled 30+ days before departure, 50% refund for 15-30 days, and no refund for cancellations within 15 days of departure. Travel insurance is available for more flexible options.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance that covers trip cancellation, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and more. You can add insurance during the booking process.

Can I customize a package?

Absolutely! Contact us and our travel experts will work with you to create a customized itinerary that matches your preferences, budget, and travel dates.
